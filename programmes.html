Our Programmes

Creative Programme

The Creative Programme encourages self-expression and creativity through workshops in painting, drama, music, creative writing, and dance. Participants are provided a safe space to explore their talents, build confidence, and develop teamwork and problem-solving skills. By the end of the programme, youth will have a portfolio of creative projects and opportunities to showcase their work in exhibitions or online platforms.

Career Programme

The Career Programme prepares youth for the workplace and future career opportunities. It includes mentorship from industry experts, CV writing workshops, interview simulations, job shadowing, and entrepreneurship sessions. Participants gain practical professional skills, career guidance, and networking opportunities, empowering them to make informed decisions about their future.

Leadership Programme

The Leadership Programme helps youth develop essential leadership and management skills through team-building exercises, public speaking, and project planning activities. Participants learn effective communication, ethical decision-making, and problem-solving, while receiving mentoring from community leaders. By the end of the programme, youth are equipped with the confidence and skills to lead initiatives and become positive role models in their communities.
